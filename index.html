Suddenly, you might feel small based on the fact that there are 259 births per minute*... But have you ever wondered how many people in this world have experienced similar things or come from the same background as you? This project aims to give you a glimpse of that knowledge based on the official world census data from the UN.

* According to UN, The 2021 average of 18.1 births per 1,000 total population equates to approximately 4.3 births per second or about 259 births per minute for the world.
